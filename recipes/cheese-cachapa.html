Cheese Cachapas

Ingredients

  1. 2 cups fresh corn, cut from the cob
  2. 1 egg
  3. 1/2 cup of milk
  4. 4 tablespoons of cornmeal
  5. 1 tablespoon of sugar
  6. 1 teaspoon of salr
  7. 2 tablespoon of butter
  8. 8 oz of sliced fresh mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Add fresh corn, egg, milk, cornmeal, sugar, and salt in a blender and blend until a thick paste forms. Let it sit for 10 minutes. Add more cornmeal if mixture is too thin
  2. Preheat a frying pan on medium heat. Add butter when it's hot
  3. Pour 1/3 cup of batter into the frying pan, in a circle shape.
  4. Cook for 4-5 minutes and flip with a spatula. Cook for 3 more minutes until the cachapa is golden brown.
  5. Place sliced mozzarella on one half of the cachapa and let it melt. Fold the other half over the cheese and spread some butter on top.
