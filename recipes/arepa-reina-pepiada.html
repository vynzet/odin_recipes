Arepa Reina Pepiada

Ingredients

For the arepas

  1. 2 cups of precooked corn flour 290 g.
  2. 2 Â¼ cups of warm water 510 ml.
  3. 1 teaspoon of salt.
  4. 2 tablespoons of oil.

For the filling

  1. 1 shredded roasted chicken breast, 300 g.
  2. 2 sprigs of cilantro.
  3. 1 small garlic clove.
  4. 1 medium onion 80 g.
  5. 1 ripe avocado 300 g.
  6. 3 tablespoons of mayonnaise
  7. 1 tablespoon of olive oil.
  8. 1 tablespoon of lemon juice
  9. Salt to taste

Steps

For the arepas

  1. Place the warm water in a mixing bowl. Then add the salt, and a tablespoon of oil, stir.
  2. Add the flour stirring constantly with a spoon. Knead until you get a nice and smooth consistency, without lumps.
  3. Divide the dough into six equal-sized balls and form the arepas by flattening them between your hands. Spread the remaining tablespoon of oil on the surface of a frying pan.
  4. Turn the stove on medium high heat, and place the arepas to cook covered, with enough space between them.
  5. When the first five minutes have passed, turn them over and cook covered for another five minutes on the opposite side. Remove them and let them rest.

For the filling

  1. Peel the avocado, reserve at least 25% of it to serve sliced inside the arepas, and mash the rest.
  2. Cut the other part of the avocado into thin slices. To prevent the avocado from turning black, you can sprinkle with a few drops of lemon juice.
  3. Finely chop the onion and cilantro. Crush the garlic clove. Place the seasonings in a bowl and mix with the olive oil, lemon juice, salt and mayonnaise.
  4. Pour the mashed avocado into the bowl with the mayonnaise and stir until a smooth paste is formed.
  5. Add the shredded chicken. Mix everything with a wooden spoon. Taste and adjust salt if necessary.
  6. With a sharp knife, open the arepas, remove the excess dough, and fill to your liking. Remember to leave a little space to place some avocado slices.
